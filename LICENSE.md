LEGACY GAME STUDIOS — DEEP‑SPACE DEVELOPMENT LICENSE (DSDL‑1.0)

A cultural, ethical, and operational charter for creators working beyond Earth.

---

1. Sovereignty Clause

Sovereignty Clause
Legacy Game Studios operates as a sovereign creative entity within the Space‑LEAF Corp ecosystem.
This sovereignty is cultural, ethical, and psychological — not political.

Core Principles

• The studio maintains creative independence while upholding Space‑LEAF values.
• All works produced under this license acknowledge the Space‑LEAF ecosystem as their cultural origin.
• No external entity may claim ownership, control, or representation of Legacy Game Studios without explicit consent.
• The studio’s identity, symbols, lore, and mission are protected from misuse, distortion, or commercial exploitation.


Purpose

To preserve the integrity of a studio designed for deep‑space psychological conditions, kid‑safe creativity, and ethical worldbuilding.

---

2. Deep‑Space Developer Protocol

Deep‑Space Developer Protocol
This protocol defines how developers should be treated when working in isolated, off‑planet, or psychologically demanding environments.

Human‑Centered Standards

• No crunch culture — isolation amplifies stress; deadlines must respect mental health.
• Flexible pacing — developers may work asynchronously across stations, time zones, or orbital cycles.
• Isolation‑aware communication — no guilt, pressure, or expectation for immediate responses.
• Environmental empathy — developers may experience silence, confinement, or sensory deprivation; all team interactions must be supportive.
• Earth‑independence — no requirement for physical presence on Earth or traditional office environments.
• Mental health priority — breaks, decompression, and psychological safety are mandatory cultural norms.


Purpose

To ensure that deep‑space development is humane, sustainable, and emotionally safe.

---

3. Kid‑Safe Requirements

Kid‑Safe Requirements
All games, demos, and tools under this license must adhere to strict safety standards.

Mandatory Rules

• No ads, tracking, or data harvesting.
• No gambling mechanics, loot boxes, or predatory monetization.
• No external scripts or unverified third‑party libraries.
• No chat systems without moderation or safety filters.
• No content that encourages harm, bullying, exploitation, or unsafe behavior.
• Clear parental visibility and transparent behavior.


Purpose

To create a safe, ethical, and transparent environment for young players and learners.

---

4. Open Demo Permission

Open Demo Permission
This license encourages open creativity while protecting studio identity.

Permissions

• Anyone may fork, study, remix, or build upon demos.
• Demos may be used for education, experimentation, or prototyping.
• Derivative works must credit Legacy Game Studios and Space‑LEAF Corp.


Restrictions

• No derivative work may claim official Legacy Game Studios branding.
• No commercial use without explicit permission.
• No removal of kid‑safe protections.


Purpose

To foster an open creative ecosystem while preserving brand integrity.

---

5. Game Pod Integration Rules

Game Pod Integration Rules
Defines how games enter the Legacy Game Studios catalogue.

Requirements

• Each game must include a manifest describing version, safety, and technical details.
• Games must be kid‑safe and follow the deep‑space protocol.
• Games must not violate sovereignty or identity protection clauses.
• Games must be stable, runnable, and free of malicious code.
• External contributors must follow studio etiquette and ethics.


Purpose

To maintain a clean, professional, and safe catalogue of games.

---

6. Proper Employee Work Etiquette & Ethics

Employee Work Ethics
This section defines how humans should treat each other inside the studio — the heart of your “humanity‑first” vision.

Ethical Standards

• Respect for isolation — understand that deep‑space developers may experience unique psychological states.
• No hostility, harassment, or pressure — emotional safety is mandatory.
• Collaborative kindness — communication should be supportive, patient, and empathetic.
• Creative sovereignty — developers retain authorship and dignity over their creations.
• Transparency — no hidden agendas, manipulative practices, or deceptive behavior.
• Accessibility — tools, documentation, and communication must be clear and inclusive.
• Cultural neutrality — no discrimination based on Earth nation, background, or identity.
• Human dignity clause — every contributor is treated as a valued human being, not a resource.


Purpose

To ensure that the studio remains a beacon of ethical creativity, psychological safety, and human-centered design.
